Ben Carter  /  The Civic Sense

Helping you build impact, income and policy reach.

I work with environmental and social impact organisations at the point where ambition needs to become financially and politically real. Most already have a strong sense of purpose. What they need is someone to step back with, think it through together, and deliver the first steps alongside them.

I work especially well with organisations at the leading edge — where the market is still being defined, the proposition is still being shaped, and the route to sustainable impact isn't yet obvious.

I love hearing about the ambitions, challenges and opportunities of people who want to make a difference and value every conversation, whether we end up working together or not.

What I offer

01

Income development & commercial activation

For organisations with ambitious plans that have stalled, a new market they can see but cannot yet reach, or a funding model that has plateaued. I identify what is actually possible, build the revenue model around it, and can deliver the first steps.

  • Income mix review and development strategy
  • New and untested market entry
  • Nature finance and Biodiversity Net Gain
  • Corporate and statutory partnership development
  • Scaling supporter and fundraising operations
02

Advocacy strategy & stakeholder positioning

For organisations that need to engage decision-makers effectively but haven't yet clarified what they are asking for, or how to present it in a way that lands. I help you find the right ask, sharpen it, and build the strategy to make it count.

  • Policy ask development and positioning
  • Stakeholder engagement strategy
  • Political and regulatory navigation
  • Public affairs campaigns and consultations
  • Communications for complex policy environments
